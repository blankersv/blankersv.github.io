I am the Director for Research Development and Collaboration in the College of Science and part-time lecturer at Northeastern University. Before that, I was a Zelevinksy RTG Postdoctoral Fellow in the mathematics department at Northeastern University after completing my dissertation in the mathematics department of Colorado State University, studying under Dr. Renzo Cavalieri. My mathematical research interests include algebraic geometry, enumerative geometry, moduli space theory, and combinatorial algebraic geometry.

For more information on my research and teaching experience use the links above.
